About me

After finishing my Master degree at the university of Bern in Switzerland (thesis under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Thomas Wihler), I started a PhD position at INRIA Paris in applied mathematics, under the supervision of Martin Vorhalik and Andre Harnist. My research area is a posteriori error estimation of nonlinear elliptic PDEs, where the focus is mainly on robustness of the estimators as well as the study of contraction and optimality of the induced adaptive finite elements algorithms.
